Naquadah

Naquadah also spelt as Naquada and Naqahdah is the primary material used to build stargates in the mod you are able to melt it into liquid Naquadah at a rate of 500mb per shard, 6800 per ore, or 2000mb per block. These Naquadah shards can be obtained through Naquadah ore found in the nether

Naquadah Alloy

To make Naquadah Alloy you need to use a fluid transposer to "fill" an iron ingot with 1000mb of liquid Naquadah. This Alloy can be used to craft parts of universe gates

Refined Naquadah

To make Refined Naquadah you need to use a fractionating still and give it liquid Naquadah, The conversion rate of liquid Naquadah to Refined Naquadah is 300mb to 200mb.

Titanium

Titanium is a metal used to craft the titanium iris for which you need 100 titanium ingots. Titanium is obtained from titanium ore, AUNIS' titanium ore can be found at the bottom of the overworld. Just like iron and gold you must smelt the ore to get Titanium ingots.

Trinium

Trinium is a metal used to craft the trinium iris for which you need 100 trinium ingots. Trinium is obtained from trinium ore, trinium ore is found in the end and can be difficult to spot due to it's white coloration. just like iron and gold you must smelt the ore to get Trinium ingots.

Molten Silicon

Molten silicon is the base used to make all blends to make it you need to melt sand into 50mb of molten silicon (per sand block).

Refined Silicon

Refined silicon is ued to craft the crystal control circuit and the white crystal. To make Refined Silicon you need to use a fractionating still, the conversion rate of molten silicon to refined silicon is 250mb to 100mb.

Blends

the mod adds 4 different blends: Light, Teleportation, Power, and Ender. They are all crafted in the Alchemical Imbuer their recipes are:

Light

200mb molten silicon and 1 glowstone dust makes 250mb of Light blend

Teleportation

200mb molten silicon and 1 lapis lazuli dust makes 250mb of teleportation blend

Power

200mb molten silicon and 1 redstone dust makes 250mb of Power blend

Ender

800mb molten silicon and 1 ender pearl dust makes 1000mb of Ender blend
