Description

Transport rings come in 2 varieties: the bog standard ancient variety (in some cases decorated with goa'uld decorations) and the Ori variety, presently in the mod only the bog standard rings have been implemented. Transport rings are a set of 5 rings that come out of the roof or the ground/floor in the mod they have an inner dimeter of 3 blocks.

Function

Rings are operated in 1 of three ways, these ways being: Ring control panel, Universe dialer rings mode, or OC (open computers). when operated you may have a maximum of 6 in rings in a network, rings can connect to each other with a maximum horizontal distance of 65 blocks (configurable) and a maximum vertical distance of 256 blocks (configurable). Transport rings do not require energy to teleport entities, and are unable to transport blocks. Transport rings are unable to function if obstructed in a 5x5x3 area offset upwards by 1 block some blocks such as LT (little tiles) structures are ignored by the rings' obstruction check.
